Peanut Butter Cookies (Vegan)

Description

This recipe makes 8-12 cookies and can be vegan if the butter is swapped out for a suitable alternative.

Ingredients

  • 4oz Crunchy peanut butter.
  • 2oz Dairy-free margarine/spread.
  • 2oz Soft brown sugar.
  • 2tbsp Golden syrup/agave syrup.
  • 1tsp Vanilla extract.
  • 4oz Self-raising flour.

Steps

  1. Cream the peanut butter, spread, sugar and syrup together.
  2. Mix in the flour and vanilla extract to form a dough; add just a splash of dairy-free milk or water if the mixture is too dry to hold together.
  3. Squeeze small handfuls of the dough into balls using your hands.
  4. Put onto a greased baking tray and flatten slightly, then flatten further with the back of a fork.
  5. Bake at 170C/160C Fan/Gas 3/325 F for 10-12 mins, or until golden brown.
  6. Cool for 5-10 mins before removing from tray and cooling on a wire rack.

Suitable for freezing.
